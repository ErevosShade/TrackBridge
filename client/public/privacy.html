Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

What Trackbridge does

Trackbridge transfers playlists between Spotify and YouTube Music on your behalf.

Data we access

We access your Spotify and YouTube playlists only to perform the transfer you request. We do not store playlist contents, track names, or any music data after your session ends.

OAuth tokens

Access tokens granted via Spotify and Google OAuth are stored in a secure server-side session for the duration of your use. They are never exposed to third parties, never sold, and are discarded when your session ends.

Data we do NOT collect

We do not collect names, emails, payment info, or any personal data. We do not use analytics, ads, or tracking cookies.

Third-party services

Trackbridge uses the Spotify Web API and YouTube Data API v3. Your use of those services is governed by Spotify's Privacy Policy and Google's Privacy Policy.
